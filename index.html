Bio

I'm a third-year PhD student at UNC-Chapel Hill, working under Mohit Bansal as a member of the MURGe-Lab and UNC NLP group. I obtained my B.S. in computer science from Villanova University in 2018 with a concentration in cognitive science.

Research Interests

My main research interest is in multimodal processing. Namely, in developing datasets and models towards the goal of grounding language in the environment. I'm also interested in the ethical consequences of NLP and have worked on developing tools for bias detection/mitigation.

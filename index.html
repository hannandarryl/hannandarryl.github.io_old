Bio

I'm a first-year PhD student at UNC-Chapel Hill, working under Mohit Bansal as a member of the NLP group. I obtained my B.S. in computer science from Villanova University in 2018, with a concentration in cognitive science.

Research Interests

My interests are in natural language processing, linguistics, and cognitive science. To be more specific, I'm interested in exploring language within the context of multimodal processing and embodiment.
